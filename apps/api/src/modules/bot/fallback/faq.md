## What we sell

We make denim jackets, jeans, t-shirts, and sweaters. We are a Bangladesh-based brand. We do not currently carry hoodies, footwear, or accessories.

## Sizing and fit

Each product page has a size guide with a body-measurement chart. For pants, we measure waist and inseam. For shirts and jackets, chest and length. If you are between sizes, our fits run true-to-size; pick the larger one for a relaxed fit.

## Returns

Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery. To return, go to the order in your account and click Request return. Pick a reason and add a photo if relevant. We refund after inspection passes. See denimisiabd.com/returns for the full policy.

## Shipping

Inside Dhaka: BDT 80. Outside Dhaka: BDT 120. Free shipping on orders over BDT 1,500. Delivery is typically 2 to 5 days.

## Payment

We accept cash on delivery (COD) only. Pay the delivery courier in cash when your order arrives. No online payment in the current version.

## Order tracking

Track any order at denimisiabd.com/track-order using your order number and email. Logged-in customers also see all orders under My account.

## Contact

Email contact@codeblock.studio. We usually reply within a few hours.

## About Denimisia

Denimisia is a Bangladesh denim and apparel brand. Every piece is designed with a focus on fit and longevity. Made for everyday wear, not seasonal trends.
